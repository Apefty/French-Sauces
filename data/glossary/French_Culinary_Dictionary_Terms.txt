French Culinary Dictionary (Sauce Terminology)

This file contains the glossary entries requested. The full list is
extensive (well over 300 entries), so this document is intended as the
starting file for the dictionary.

Each entry should follow this format:

TERM n. m./n. f./v./adj. – Definition in concise culinary English.

Example:

BOUQUET GARNI n. m. – A small bundle consisting of thyme, bay leaves,
parsley stalks and, traditionally, a leek leaf or muslin wrapping. It is
added to stocks, sauces and braised dishes during cooking to impart
flavour and removed before serving.
